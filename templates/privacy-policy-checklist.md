# Privacy Policy Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure your privacy policy covers GDPR requirements.

## Required Elements

- [ ] **Identity of the controller** — Company name, address, contact details
- [ ] **DPO contact** — Data Protection Officer details (if applicable)
- [ ] **Purposes of processing** — Why you collect and process personal data
- [ ] **Legal basis** — Consent, legitimate interest, contract, legal obligation, etc.
- [ ] **Categories of data** — What personal data you collect
- [ ] **Recipients** — Who you share data with (third parties, processors)
- [ ] **International transfers** — If data leaves the EEA, what safeguards apply
- [ ] **Retention periods** — How long you keep data
- [ ] **Data subject rights** — Right to access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection
- [ ] **Right to withdraw consent** — How to withdraw consent
- [ ] **Right to lodge complaint** — With supervisory authority
- [ ] **Automated decision-making** — If applicable, logic and significance
- [ ] **Source of data** — If not collected directly from the data subject
- [ ] **Statutory/contractual requirement** — If providing data is obligatory

## Third-Party Services to Document

Use ETALON scan results to identify all third-party services that should be listed:

- [ ] Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- [ ] Advertising pixels (Facebook, Google Ads, etc.)
- [ ] Chat widgets (Intercom, Zendesk, etc.)
- [ ] Error tracking (Sentry, etc.)
- [ ] Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
- [ ] CDN providers (Cloudflare, etc.)
- [ ] Social media integrations

## Best Practices

- [ ] Written in clear, plain language
- [ ] Easily accessible from every page (footer link)
- [ ] Dated with last-update timestamp
- [ ] Versioned (keep history of changes)
- [ ] Available in relevant languages
